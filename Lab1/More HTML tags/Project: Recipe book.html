Recipe #1

  • Time: 35 min
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
self-raising flour 200 g
baking powder 1 tsp
egg 1
milk 300ml
pack blueberry 150g

Step 1: Mix together 200g self-raising flour, 1 tsp baking powder and a pinch of salt in a large bowl.

Step 2: Beat 1 egg with 300ml milk, make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients and whisk in the milk to make a thick smooth batter.
